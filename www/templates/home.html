About ESC



ESC has been designing and producing sheet piles since the late 1980's, and is now a leading manufacturer of cold rolled/formed sheet piles, supplying a global network. ESC has expanded significantly into tubular piles/mono-piles, tie rod systems and structural steel through the last 10 years.

Further to simply supplying products we at ESC take a different approach to piling which is orientated on the customers requirements. ESC believes that simply supplying a product is insufficient, therefore the level of support that customers receive when they are considering purchasing ESC products is unprecedented. This ranges from general advice on the Client's options to full engineering support and design, and includes necessary accessories such as pinting, tie rods and other items associated with sheet piling works. ESC refers to this as a piling solution.

A piliting solution is a performance based package that includes the entire elements essential to provide the client with a complete solution to their sheet pile requirements, rather than just supplying a product.

Simply put, rater than just selling piles, ESC considers the Client's needs from all aspects, and then provide a solution to meet those needs. This may include engineering design, corrosion control, construction issues, planning or simply general advice.

FAST FACTS

  • ESC piles are produced in accordance with the latest international standards as well as ISO 9000 quality management systems. Certain other standards can be applied on request.
  • ESC has designed and supplied sheet piles to projects in every continent of the world.
  • ESC has established working relationships with manufacters of stell, paints, cathodic protection system s and all other items usually associated with piling works, in order to provide complete packages to customers.
  • For every project there is more than one solution alternative. You are welcome to contact your nearest ESC office or representative to get a free solution advice.
